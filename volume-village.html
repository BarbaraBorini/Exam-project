About

Volume Village stems from the Aarhus Volume Cultural Association , which was started by young people and has organized and held festivals, concerts and other events since 2016. The association is a community where young people with high ambitions have taken cultural development and community into their own hands.

This dedication to bold community action makes Volume Village an ideal space for expressive underground music. It provides a raw, focused environment for organizers to host diverse techno events. At Volume Village, the focus is on shared energy, pushing boundaries, and making genuine connections through music.
