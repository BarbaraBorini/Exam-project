INSTITUT FOR X

About

Institute for X is not a typical venue, it is a groundbreaking cultural melting pot and non-profit association established in 2009.


Located in the Godsbanen area, X functions as a self-managed ecosystem, providing a home for workshops, businesses, and public park space, all built around a philosophy of fostering creativity and pushing the boundaries of urban development.


Diverse techno events are hosted here, specifically in the A-Huset salon, organized by associations like FEUM, ACME, SOLACE and LYC, who aim to support, create and promote electronic underground music.
