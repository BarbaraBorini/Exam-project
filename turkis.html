TURKIS

About

Turkis is a non-profit musical meeting place in Aarhus, dedicated to expanding cultural horizons and breathing new life into traditions. As a collaborative and open house, Turkis works closely with many cultural actors locally and nationally, creating a space where new communities can flourish.


For the techno scene, this collaborative spirit attracts organizers seeking a professional, yet intimate setting for electronic events.


Turkis's mission is aligned with the techno community's values of unity and growth. By providing a inspiring platform, Turkis helps organizations like FEUM and others host events that emphasize musical quality, mutual respect, and the cultivation of new, vibrant communities on the dance floor.
