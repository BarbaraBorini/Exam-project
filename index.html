Community

The heart of the scene

The heart of the city's electronic scene doesn't live in typical clubs, it's build by a network of cultural associations driven by a mission: to create safe and creative spaces for expression.


Institute for X, Volume Village, and Turkis share this philosophy: They form the backbone of the underground community. They are non-profit, cultural associations, focused on fostering genuine connection.


To join the scene is to join the community. Embrace the culture.

Attitude Code

Respect is Non-Negotiable

We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, discrimination, or abuse based on race, gender, sexuality, ethnicity, or any other form of self-expression.

Always check for consent. No unwanted attention, staring, or touching. Respect everyone's physical and personal boundaries.

Stay Safe

Know your limits, consume responsibly, and stay hydrated.

Express Freely

The dancefloor is a space for movement and connection. Please limit phone use, and avoid filming or taking photos without consent to ensure everyone feels free to express themselves.

Leave No Trace

Treat the venues like your own home. Use trash bins and let crew members know if you spot any issues.
